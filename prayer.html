Prayer to Lord Ganesha

O Vakratunda,

Vanquish Matsarasura (Jealousy),

free my heart from envy's rue.

O Ekadanta,

Vanquish Madasura (Intoxication of Power),

humble pride's embrace.

O Mahodara,

Vanquish Mohasura (Delusion),

guide me to wisdom's light.

O Gajanana,

Vanquish Lobhasura (Greed),

let generosity be my key.

O Lambodara,

Vanquish Krodhasura (Anger),

grant me patience anew.

O Vikata,

Vanquish Kamasura (Lust),

help me reach a higher goal.

O Vighnaraja,

Vanquish Mamasura (Attachment),

teach me to hold life light.

O Dhumravarna,

Vanquish Ahamkarasura (Self-Infatuation),

help me leave my ego behind.
